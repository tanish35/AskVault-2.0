2B. "VIDEOPLUS ENDORSED PLUS CONTRACTOR" Program

- This program is a key differentiator and trust signal for customers.
- What it means: The PLUS sign (+) indicates that Videoplus has a strong working relationship with the chosen independent contractor (ACT/transcriber).

- Administrative Role: Videoplus handles the administrative work for the independent contractor.
- Order Processing: Videoplus processes the customer's order.
- Quality Assurance: Ensures the transcriber is well-versed and has expertise with project-specific needs.
- Communication: Videoplus keeps the customer updated on the order's progress.
