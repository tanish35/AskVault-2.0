ABOUT SECTION:
Hello, I'm Jessica Campbell, an Authorized Court Transcriptionist proudly endorsed as a Videoplus Endorsed Plus Contractor. 
With nearly a decade of experience preparing certified court transcripts, I specialize in criminal court appeals and 11(b) applications, 
ensuring every record is produced with precision, professionalism, and full compliance with court requirements and regulations.

My journey in the legal field began from a deep appreciation for writing, reading, and the law—passions that have evolved into a 
rewarding career centered on accuracy, organization, and trust. Over the years, I've also served as a Court Reporter and Court and Client 
Representative for the Ministry of the Attorney General, assisted in teaching the Authorized Court Transcriptionist course, and worked in 
management overseeing projects for the Court of Appeal.

What sets my work apart is my commitment to excellence and efficiency. I take pride in accommodating any turnaround time, streamlining the 
transcription process, and maintaining the highest level of confidentiality and professionalism. No matter the size or complexity of the 
project, I approach each transcript with care and attention to detail—ensuring my clients receive reliable, high-quality work every time.
